Biography

I am a tech leader at Aibee Inc., where our goal is to bring the offline world online. Towards this goal, I lead a team solving various in-the-wild problems, such as Face Recognition, Person Re-Identification, Fine-Grained Object Recognition, Object Detection and Object Segmentation.

Before I was leading the Fine-Grained Object Recognition direction at Baidu Research in Sunnyvale and Media Analytics group, NEC Lab in Cupertino. In 2014, I received the PhD in Robotics from Carnegie Mellon University, where I worked with Dr. Fernando de la Torre in the Human Sensing Lab. During my PhD study, I did two internships, one at Adobe's Advanced Technology Labs at San Jose, and the other in Interactive Visual Media group, Microsoft Research at Redmond.
