More Results of Temporal Clustering

Introduction

This page shows the resulting segmentation of 14 sequences performed by subject 86 in Carnegie Mellon University Motion Capture database. We compared clustering performance of three methods: Aligned Cluster Analysis (ACA)[2] Hierarchical Aligned Cluster Analysis (HACA)[1] and GMM[3]. The ground truth label is available here.
